# Integrated Business Intelligence & HR Analytics Suite

A multi-disciplinary data science repository demonstrating predictive modeling across Human Resources and E-commerce operations.

##  Core Modules

### 1. E-Commerce Customer Intent Predictor
Analyzes online shopper behavior to predict conversion and revenue generation.
* **Objective:** Identify "Likely to Purchase" sessions to optimize marketing spend.
* **Key Metrics:** PageValues, ExitRates, and Administrative Duration.
* **Files:** `5.ipynb`, `5.csv`, `5.png`

### 2. Digital Recruitment & Hiring Auditor
Automates candidate screening using psychometric and interview data.
* **Impact:** Saves ~200 manual hours per recruitment cycle.
* **Files:** `4 (1).ipynb`, `4 (1).csv`, `4 (1).png`, `4 (2).png`

### 3. Employee Attrition & Retention Engine
High-precision turnover forecasting with an integrated DEI bias audit.
* **Performance:** 93.09% ROC-AUC | 85.59% F1-Score.
* **Files:** `2.ipynb`, `2.csv`, `2.png` through `2 (5).png`

### 4. Compensation Benchmarking
Regression modeling for fair and competitive salary structuring.
* **Files:** `3.ipynb`, `3.csv`, `3.png`

##  Technical Stack
- **Languages:** Python (Jupyter Notebooks)
- **ML Libraries:** Scikit-Learn, Imbalanced-Learn (SMOTE)
- **Data Handling:** Pandas, NumPy
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn

##  Strategic Value
This suite provides a 360-degree view of business health—from selecting and retaining the right talent to understanding the digital behavior of the end customer.
